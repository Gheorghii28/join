What is Join?

Join is a project management methodology that originated in the manufacturing industry and has since been adopted in various fields, including software development, product management, and other knowledge-based projects. At its core, Join is focused on visualizing work and workflow to improve efficiency, productivity, and collaboration within a team. It uses a visual board, often divided into columns, to represent different stages of work. Tasks or work items are represented by cards or sticky notes, which are moved across the board as they progress through the workflow.

How to use it

1.

Visualize the workflow: Create a visual representation of the work stages, typically using a Join board, to provide a clear overview of the tasks and their progress.

2.

Limit work in progress (WIP): Set explicit limits on the number of tasks that can be in progress simultaneously. This helps prevent overloading the team and promotes a focus on completing work before starting new tasks.

3.

Manage flow: Aim to have a smooth and continuous flow of work through the system, avoiding bottlenecks and minimizing delays.

4.

Make policies explicit: Clearly define the rules, policies, and criteria for moving tasks through different stages. This ensures transparency and consistency in the workflow.

5.

Continuously improve: Encourage ongoing reflection, analysis, and optimization of the workflow and processes to achieve higher efficiency and quality.
